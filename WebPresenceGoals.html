Some of My Goals:



Goals are an idea of the future or desired result that a person or a group of people envisions, plans and commits to achieve. People endeavor to reach goals within a finite time by setting deadlines. a few of my goals (short term) are to graduate which should occur in a couple of weeks, get a job in hopefully Florida, Georgia, or North Carolina! Long term goals are to buy a house, a new car, and possibly have a family! I have taken many classes where we have learned about smart goals which are: specific, measurable, achieveable, relevant, and time based.
